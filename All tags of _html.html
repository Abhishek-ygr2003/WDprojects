Virat Kohli is an Indian cricketer and former captain of the Indian national cricket team. He was born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, India.

Kohli is widely regarded as one of the best batsmen in the world. He has achieved numerous records and accolades throughout his career. Known for his aggressive playing style and consistency, Kohli has been the backbone of Indian batting line-up for many years.

Kohli made his international debut in August 2008 and has since represented India in all three formats of the game. He has scored over 20,000 international runs across formats, including numerous centuries and double centuries. He holds several records, including the fastest century in One Day Internationals (ODIs) and the fastest player to score 8,000, 9,000, 10,000, 11,000, and 12,000 runs in ODIs.

In addition to his international success, Kohli has also been a key player in the Indian Premier League (IPL). He has captained the Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB) franchise since 2013. Kohli is known for his exceptional leadership skills and passion for the game.

Off the field, Kohli is involved in various philanthropic activities and is considered a role model for aspiring cricketers around the world. He has received numerous awards and recognition for his contributions to the sport.

Virat Kohli's dedication, skill, and passion for cricket have made him a true icon of the game. He continues to inspire millions of fans and aspiring cricketers worldwide.
